About us:

This is a project by a group of students enrolled in Communications Lab (Summer). As part of the assignment, we were required to produce a video in thirty minutes and individually make a website by implementing the skills we have learned in this class. Our aim was to make a video to encourage people towards the ways they can spend their quarantine during COVID productive.

What is the Thirty Minute Film Festival?

The very first activity of this course is called the 'Thirty Minute Film Festival' , that required us to make a video in thirty minutes using our creativity spontaneously by working in a team remotely from four different areas of the world and coming together with content that depicted a story.
